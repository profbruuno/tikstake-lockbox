TikStake White Paper

TikStake is a non-custodial-inspired crypto holding platform for users who want to lock their assets for up to 20 years, without expecting profits or yield. Our service is simple: choose your asset and lock duration, send funds to a generated address, and provide your transaction proof and next of kin. TikStake keeps your crypto safely until your chosen unlock date.

Unlike lending, staking, or investment platforms, TikStake does not use your assets to generate returns or take risks on your behalf. We do not support stablecoins, focusing instead on major cryptocurrencies. Each lock is isolated and traceable, and users receive a digital receipt for every deposit. If you are unable to reclaim your asset, your designated next of kin can do so with the provided information and proof.

TikStake is designed for personal savings, inheritance, or secure asset planning. We believe in security, transparency, and giving you control of your crypto legacy—no profit promises, just peace of mind.
